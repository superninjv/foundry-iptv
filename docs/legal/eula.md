### 5.3 User Control

The managed update service is **optional and can be disabled** at any time through the Product settings. You may:

- Enable automatic updates (default for hardware appliance).
- Enable manual update notifications only.

### 6.2 Opt-In Analytics

You may choose to enable anonymous usage analytics. If opted in, the following data may be collected:
